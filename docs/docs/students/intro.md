# FAILS: Student Guide


Welcome to your guide for using the Fancy Automated Internet Lecture System (FAILS). FAILS is an open-source, browser-based platform designed with data privacy and security at its core.

## 📚 What Students Can Do With FAILS
FAILS allows students to move beyond traditional note-taking by integrating interactive tools directly into the lecture environment:
*   **Real-time Participation:** Participate in live polls and submit questions via chat.
Use interactive Jupyter lite based applets or directly edit the Jupyter notebook discussed in the lecture.
*   **Digital Note-Taking:** Draw personal notes, annotate slides, and save work locally on your device.
*   **Accessibility:** Follow the lecturer's content (blackboard or slides) on any modern tablet, phone, or computer browser.
*   **Offline Learning:** Automatically generate downloadable PDFs for exam preparation and study.

## 🚀 Getting Started: Joining the Lecture
FAILS is fully integrated into your school's LMS (Learning Management System).

1.  **Access Course:** Log in to your institution’s LMS (e.g., Moodle).
2.  **Select Activity:** Click on the FAILS activity for the current lesson or lecture.
3.  **Join Lecture:** On the main panel, click the **`Join lecture` button**. The lecture content will appear on your device screen.

### Downloadable Resources
Before or after the lecture, you can generate and download comprehensive notes:
*   **PDF Color:** Downloads a full-color version of all displayed lecture material.
*   **PDF Black & White:** Downloads a streamlined black and white version optimized for printing.
