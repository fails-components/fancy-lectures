# Private Notes and Content Tracking

Students have full control over their personal note-taking experience while minimizing interference with the core lecture content.

## ✏️ Drawing and Personal Annotations (Notes Mode)
By entering "Notes Taking" mode, you create a private workspace directly on your device's notepad:
*   **Tools Available:** You get access to the Pen tool (with color/size options), Marker tool for emphasis, Eraser, Undo button, and Magic Wand.
*   **Workflow:** Draw personal notes or mark important concepts directly onto the blackboard using these tools during the lecture.
*   **Content Tracking:** When you are actively drawing in Notes Taking mode, the system automatically unlocks the scrolling function, preventing the lecturer from interfering with your private annotations.

### Persistence of Notes (Critical Step)
By default, notes taken in this private mode are stored locally on your device and *not* uploaded to the school server. To ensure permanent saving:
Add the FAILS webpage as a favorite or install it as an application (**PWA**) to make all subsequent annotations permanently saveable.

### Following Content Movement (Auto-Scrolling)
The system is designed to track your view of the lecture material:
*   **Automatic Follow:** When the lecturer scrolls the blackboard, your device automatically follows this movement, ensuring you never lose sight of the content.
*   **Manual Control:** You can manually control this feature using the **lock button**:
    *   **Locked (Default):** Your view automatically tracks the board's scrolling position.
    *   **Unlocked:** Allows free manual scrolling using the arrow keys, giving you independent navigation while still remaining relative to the classroom blackboard area.

If a presentation is shared via screen sharing, you can also manually or automatically switch between the black board or the shared screen.
