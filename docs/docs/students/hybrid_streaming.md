# Hybrid Learning & Multimedia Participation

FAILS supports modern hybrid learning models, allowing students to participate safely and seamlessly from home or other remote locations via dedicated streaming features.

## 🌐 Joining the Live Session (Hybrid Access)
If attending remotely, you can join the lecture using the specialized A/V panel:

1.  **Joining:** Click the **"Join lecture" button** to activate the connection.
2.  **Viewing Feeds:** After joining the lecture, you have to activate **Audio/Video streaming** using a button. Once connected to the network of audio/video routers, the system allows streaming of both video and audio feeds from the lecturer's location.

### Student Control Panel (Student-Controlled Elements)
The student panel provides independent control over your viewing experience:
*   **Display Toggle:** Turn the video feed On or Off to manage bandwidth.
*   **Speaker Output:** Controls local audio output.
*   **Microphone/Camera:** These can be activated if you need to speak or show visual evidence during the lecture session.

### Security and Data Integrity
The streaming system uses advanced protocols to guarantee student safety and privacy:
*   **End-to-End Encryption (E2EE):** All audio and video transmissions are secured by E2EE, ensuring that third parties cannot intercept or monitor the content.
*   **Pseudonymized Routing:** The routing protocol obscures connection details, preventing identification of students or specific lectures being broadcasted.

## 🖥️ Advanced Viewing Modes
FAILS seamlessly handles shifts between different presentation types:
*   **Combined Display Switching:** If the lecturer shares a PowerPoint/Keynote presentation *and* uses the blackboard, FAILS automatically manages the display by switching focus between these two views.
*   **Manual Control:** Students can use an **unlock button** to manually dictate whether the displayed view should prioritize the formal slides or the live blackboard drawing.

## 🖼️ Viewing Notes
The system ensures that all course materials are readily available for review:
*   **Viewing Assignment Material:** The platform integrates uploaded pictures and notes directly into the lecture timeline, allowing students to see context-specific visual aids as they are discussed.
